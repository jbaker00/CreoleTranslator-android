Creole Translator is a fast, accurate voice translation app for Haitian Creole and English speakers.

🎙️ SPEAK — Just tap and talk. Your voice is transcribed and translated instantly.
🔄 TWO DIRECTIONS — Translate from Haitian Creole to English or English to Haitian Creole with one tap.
🔊 LISTEN — Tap the speaker icon to hear the translation read aloud in the target language.
📋 HISTORY — Review all your past translations and replay them anytime.

HOW IT WORKS
Creole Translator uses state-of-the-art AI (Whisper speech recognition + Llama translation) to deliver natural, fluent translations — not stiff word-for-word substitutions.

PERFECT FOR
• Haitian families communicating across language barriers
• Healthcare workers, social workers, and educators serving Haitian communities
• Travelers visiting Haiti
• Anyone learning Haitian Creole

PRIVACY
Audio is processed securely and is not stored after translation. See our privacy policy for full details.
