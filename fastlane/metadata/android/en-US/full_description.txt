Translate between Haitian Creole (Kreyòl) and English instantly — by voice or by typing. Powered by state-of-the-art AI, Haitian Creole Translator delivers natural, fluent translations, not awkward word-for-word substitutions.

🎙️ SPEAK — Tap to record. Your voice is transcribed and translated in seconds.
⌨️ TYPE — Prefer typing? Enter any text and get an instant Creole or English translation.
🔄 TWO DIRECTIONS — Switch between Creole→English and English→Creole with a single tap.
🔊 LISTEN — Hear translations read aloud with natural-sounding text-to-speech.
📋 HISTORY — Every translation is saved locally so you can review and replay anytime.

HOW IT WORKS
Haitian Creole Translator uses Whisper (OpenAI's speech recognition model) for accurate voice transcription and Llama 3 for natural-language translation. The result sounds like a human translated it — not a dictionary lookup.

PERFECT FOR
• Haitian families bridging language barriers with English-speaking relatives
• Doctors, nurses, and healthcare workers treating Haitian patients
• Social workers, teachers, and community organizers in Haitian communities
• Travelers and missionaries visiting Haiti
• Students learning Haitian Creole (Kreyòl ayisyen)
• Anyone who needs quick Creole-to-English or English-to-Creole translation

WHY HAITIAN CREOLE TRANSLATOR
Most translation apps treat Haitian Creole as an afterthought. This app was built specifically for Kreol speakers and the people who work with them. Voice input means you don't have to know how to spell in Creole — just speak naturally.

PRIVACY
Audio is sent securely to AI services for transcription and translation, then discarded. Nothing is stored on our servers. Full details in our privacy policy.
